About Me

Born in Mexico but raised in the beautiful White Mountains of north-eastern Arizona, Saul has called the great state 48 home for over 25 years. Growing up in the mountains Saul grew fond of the outdoors and enjoys activities such as fishing, hiking, camping and kayaking. When not working or stuck in traffic, you can find him spending time with his wife and kids or watching a game. At the present moment however he is too busy trying to learn HTML, CSS, Javascript and all that other mumbo jumbo the UofA convinced him was so great.
